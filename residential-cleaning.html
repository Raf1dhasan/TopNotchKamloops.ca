Residential Cleaning

At Top Notch, we believe that a clean home should be accessible and affordable, without unnecessary fees. That’s why our residential cleaning services offer flexible packages at fair, competitive prices to match your unique needs and budget.

Our Cleaning Packages

Standard Cleaning Package

Ideal for maintaining a fresh and welcoming home, our Standard Cleaning Package includes all essential tasks to keep your main living areas in top condition. We focus on vacuuming, dusting, mopping, and wiping down surfaces, ensuring each room looks its best. This package is designed for regular upkeep, leaving your home consistently clean without the high price tag often charged for routine services.

Deep Clean Package

If your home needs extra attention, our Deep Clean Package delivers. This thorough service tackles often-overlooked spaces like baseboards, ceiling fans, light fixtures, and the interiors of appliances such as ovens and refrigerators. Our team doesn’t just clean around surfaces but gets into those hard-to-reach spots to leave every corner of your home spotless.

Move-In/Out Cleaning Package

Moving is stressful enough without worrying about cleaning up before or after. Our Move-In/Out Cleaning Package ensures that your property is ready for new occupants, whether it’s for a move-in, move-out, or to refresh an empty home for the next chapter. We focus on comprehensive cleaning of every room, ensuring cabinets, closets, bathrooms, and floors are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized.
