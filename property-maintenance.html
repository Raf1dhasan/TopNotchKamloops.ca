Property Maintenance & Small Renovations

Keeping a property well-maintained requires time and effort. At Top Notch, we offer a suite of property maintenance and small renovation services designed to keep your property in prime condition year-round. From regular upkeep to special projects, our experienced team handles it all—so you don’t have to.

Our Property Maintenance Services


Landscaping & Yard Maintenance

Your property’s exterior is the first impression visitors get, and we help make it a great one. Our landscaping and yard maintenance services include:

  • Routine lawn care such as mowing, trimming, and fertilizing
  • Seasonal planting, mulching, and landscape design to enhance curb appeal
  • Leaf cleanup in the fall and snow removal during winter months for a well-kept look throughout the year
  • Customizable plans based on your property’s specific needs and climate

Whether you need weekly upkeep or seasonal overhauls, our team ensures your outdoor spaces remain vibrant and inviting.

Minor Property Renovations

Small repairs and updates can make a big difference in the value and appeal of your property. Our minor renovation services cover:

  • Painting and wall patching to refresh or update interior and exterior spaces
  • Repairing minor damage, including drywall, floorboards, and door or window frames
  • Upgrading fixtures, hardware, or lighting to modernize and improve functionality
  • Customizable renovation projects tailored to meet your specific property goals

Ideal for homeowners looking to maintain or enhance their property’s appeal, our renovation services are handled efficiently and affordably, adding both value and style.
